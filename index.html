Frequently Asked Questions

What is a percentage?

A percentage is a number or ratio expressed as a fraction of 100. It's commonly used to compare values, express discounts, and show changes.

How do I calculate a percentage?

To find X% of Y, multiply X by Y and divide by 100. This tool does the math for you instantly.

How is percentage difference calculated?

The percentage difference is the absolute difference between two values, divided by their average, then multiplied by 100.

Can I use this tool for discounts?

Yes! Just enter the original price and discount percent — it will show you the final price instantly.
